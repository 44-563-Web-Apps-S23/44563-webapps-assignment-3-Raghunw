Red Reindeer

Reindeer are large deer, with a thick coat that is brown during the summer, and during the winter it is gray. They have a pale-colored chest and undersides, with their rump and tail being white. Males and females both have antlers, with those of males being larger and more complex.

  1. They can fly
  2. Their eyes change colour like living sunglasses, from gold in summer to blue in winter
  3. Produce lots of vitamin D
